Friends Home Visitors
  • 1: Resort Plaza

    This is where majority of the shops are and is the heart of the island.

  • 2: Resort Gate

    Chocolate and his crafting table is found here.

  • 3: Cozy Island

    A romantic section of islands that houses two visitors.

  • 4: Hopscotch Islands

    A chain of varying islands that are hops away from each other.

  • 5: Moon Island

    Cresent-shape island that holds a portal once unlocked.

  • 6: Spooky Swamp

    It's always dark here and has pumpkins galore.

  • 7: Nature Preserve

    Hosts many bodies of water and has the bug museum.

  • 8: Ghost Tour Ride

    A spooky train ride with ghosts that pop out at you.

  • 9: Gemstone Mountain

    Like the Wild West but more cute.

  • 10: Oasis

    Hosts the ice cream boat and Sakura trees blossom here.

  • 11: Ice Peak Entrance

    Entrance to a windy and cold area.

  • 12: Kelp Maze

    Long kelp leaves hide many treasures here.

  • 13: Comedy Club

    Underwater club with piping hot espresso.

  • 14: Sunken Ship

    Very deep dive to a sunken ship that has chocolate coins.

  • 15: Mount Hothead

    An active volcano that requires a lot of agility to climb.

  • 16: Hot Springs

    A beautiful springs with views of the volcano.

  • 17: Caldera

    The top of the volcano with a large pit of lava.

  • 18: Icy Peak

    The highest place in the game and is frozen in time.

  • 19: Cloud Island Plaza

    Up in the clouds, the Twins live here and watch over the island.
