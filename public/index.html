What is Hello Kitty Island Adventure?

Hello Kitty Island Adventure is a social simulation game that is on Apple Arcade.

The goal is to have your "vibe check" of your island be 5 stars and become BFFs with all of the Sanrio crew. It is an open world with different climates ranging from desert, underwater, and tundra.

The main storyline is that you (in Sanrio form) and the Sanrio crew are on vacation on an island. The island has a mysterious story that as you play and interact, you discover it.

There is no money, just exchanging of gifts. You create different gifts by baking, crafting, making pizzas, whipping up cotton candy, brewing coffee products, and bottling up sodas. You get these ingredients by collecting items around the world and receiving gifts when you gift them to your friends.

You can decorate cabins to visitor's specifications and if you build your friendship with them, they will ask to stay.

Hello Kitty Island Adventure is a long-range dynamic game and is always adding new updates for various seasons.

Make best friends forever and work your way up to the best island!
